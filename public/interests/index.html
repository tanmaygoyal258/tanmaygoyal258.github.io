Research Interests

I usually like to work at the intersection of theory and application, with an emphasis on the former. I am interested in the following areas:

  • Sequential Decision Making with a focus on Bandits and Online Learning
  • Statistical Learning Theory
  • Theoretical foundations of Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning
  • Entangling (pun intended!) Classical Learning with Quantum resources, and designing efficient algorithms for Quantum Learning
